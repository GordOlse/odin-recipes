Lasagna

The best lasagna ever

If you want the best lasagna ever, see to the ingredients and instructions below!

ingredients

  • Noodles
  • Cottage Cheese
  • Cheddar Cheese
  • Ground Sasuage
  • Enchilada Sauce

Cooking Instructions

  1. Pre Heat Oven to 375
  2. Cook Sausage and drain oil
  3. Layer in ingredients
  4. Cook for 30 minutes
  5. Scarf!
