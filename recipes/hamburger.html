Hamburger

The best Hamburger ever

If you want the best Hamburger ever, see to the ingredients and instructions below!

ingredients

  • Buns
  • Sharp Cheddar Cheese
  • egg
  • spicies
  • Ground Turkey
  • BBQ Sauce
  • Jalepeno
  • Lettuce

Cooking Instructions

  1. Pre grill to 375
  2. Prep ground turkey into 1/3 lb patties
    • mix in egg and spice to taste
  3. cook patties to your liking, medium well for me
  4. add Cheese on top to melt
  5. Dress up your bun with condiments
  6. top with lettuce and Jalepeno
  7. Scarf!
