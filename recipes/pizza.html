Pizza

The best pizza ever

If you want the best pizza ever, see to the ingredients and instructions below!

ingredients

  • Dough
  • Cheddar Cheese
  • Pepperoni
  • Marinara Sauce

Cooking Instructions

  1. Pre Heat Oven to 425
  2. Prepare Dough
  3. add Marinara sauce to dough
  4. add Cheese
  5. add Pepperoni
  6. cook until crust is golden brown
  7. Slice and Scarf!
