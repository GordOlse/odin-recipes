Pizza

Description

Pizza is a dish of Italian origin consisting of a usually round, flat base of leavened wheat-based dough topped with tomatoes, cheese, and often various other ingredients (such as various types of sausage, anchovies, mushrooms, onions, olives, vegetables, meat, ham, etc.), which is then baked at a high temperature, traditionally in a wood-fired oven.[1]

Ingredients

  • Dough
  • Marinara Sauce
  • Cheese
  • Candandian Bacon
  • Pineapple

Steps

  1. Start with a crust
  2. Add a sauce
  3. Add Cheese
  4. Add Canadian Bacon
  5. Add Pineapple
  6. Add more cheese
  7. Bake at 425 for 20 minutes
  8. Let cool, eat!
